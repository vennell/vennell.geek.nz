Hello everyone, my name is Ben Vennell and I am a Geek.

Together with the support of my Wife, Erin, and our Son, Alex, I have been able to channel this crippling affliction into a useful skill. Unfortunately for that to continue I need a release. For the past few years I have found a substitute to spending my money on new toys and systems, I now spend other peoples and in the process get them the new gadgets that I can no longer trust myself with.
