Mission

Personal computers have become a major part of peoples lives. As such, good support for these devices is a must. Traditionally people understood the need to know a mechanic, a builder and a plumber. We should all now have a computer technician on that list.

As an after hours activity I have been making my technical services available for those who need advice or technical assistance with computer related issues.

If you require assistance with anything computer related please send me an email.
